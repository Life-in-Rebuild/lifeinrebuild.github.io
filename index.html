Life in Rebuild

Life in Rebuild is a slow-living media project about rebuilding an old estate by the sea— with equal parts craft, chaos, and calm. It's what happens when you trade modern convenience for timber, time, and an increasingly unmanageable collection of half-finished projects and emotional support extension cords.

It's a celebration of imperfection, reclaimed wood, and solving problems you definitely caused yourself.

Follow along as we attempt to rebuild a life using patience, questionable screws, and an unreasonable number of YouTube tutorials—under the ever-watchful gaze of a deer who’s clearly seen better renovation attempts—and isn't impressed.
